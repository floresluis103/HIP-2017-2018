Project Proposal

Autonomous Car

Overall Description/Summary

Develop a small model of a car, in which the car can drive on its own, and perform functions such as stop, go, turn, and park without significant human manipulation.

Goals/Capabilities/Functionalities
  • • Have the car understand movement of cars around it, so if a car is approaching an object, it stops until it senses that the object is further away.
  • • Understand traffic, understands speed limits, and understands traffic lights.
  • • Self-Parking
  • • Detect day time or night time
  • • Understanding when to turn right or left.
  • • Use lights to indicate movement (left, right, brake)
Ways to scale back project
  • • Wireless connection between car and track
  • • Detect day time or night time
  • • Lights around car
  • • Parallel Parking
Reach goals for project
  • • Self-parking
  • • Wireless connection
  • • Manual override
  • • Speed limit
