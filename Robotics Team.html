About Me

Robotics Team

I am the captain of the robotics team at Canoga Park High School. Currently building a robot that will compete in the First Robotics Competition in March.

Our robot is named Silvester, and hopefully will be a really solid and successful robot

The goal of the competition is to score more points than your opponent by keeping possesion of the switches and scale.

The robot also has a final task of being able to lift itself 12 inches off of the floor at the end on the competition.

I have great faith in my team and believe the Power Circuits will have a good run in the competition.
